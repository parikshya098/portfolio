Writing Projects:


Foodie Narrative

This narrative describes my festive food experience during Dashain, a major festival in Nepal. It vividly portrays the cultural significance of the festival, highlighting traditional Nepali cuisine and family gatherings at my grandparents' old mud house. The detailed description of the preparation, the variety of dishes, and the communal eating style captures the essence of Nepalese culinary traditions and family bonds. This piece not only reflects as my personal memory but also serves as a rich exposition of Nepali culture through food.


14 Peaks - Nothing is Impossible

This document analyzes the documentary "14 Peaks: Nothing Is Impossible," which follows Nepali mountaineer Nirmal Purja's extraordinary quest to climb all 14 of the world's 8,000-meter mountains in just seven months. It highlights the physical challenges and bureaucratic hurdles he faced, alongside showcasing the broader implications for recognition and support of Nepali climbers in the mountaineering community. The narrative combines elements of adventure, perseverance, and the power of human spirit, making it an inspiring account of breaking boundaries and achieving the seemingly impossible.

Currency Value History

This project is a web-based application that tracks the historical value changes of different currencies over selected date ranges. Users can choose a base currency and a target currency, specify a date range, and the app displays a line graph showing how the exchange rate has varied over that period. Capstone I - Project Charter Plan

The project charter plan outlines the development of a UV Index Tracker for the Southern Skin Cancer Clinic of America. The goal is to create a webpage that helps users monitor UV exposure and make informed decisions to protect their skin. The tracker will feature a user-friendly interface with real-time and forecasted UV levels, displayed on a color-coded chart. It will be designed with privacy in mind, adhering to HIPAA regulations to secure user data. The project aims to enhance the clinic's services by integrating this innovative tool.
